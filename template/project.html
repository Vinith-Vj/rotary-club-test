Our Projects

Rotary Club of Tangasseri and NS Cooperative Hospital Conduct Life-Changing Cleft Palate Surgeries for Tribal Children.
The Rotary Club of Tangasseri and the NS Cooperative Hospital joined hands to conduct a major Cleft Palate surgery for two children, Sumesh aged 15 years and Arshida mol aged 9 years of the Achankovil tribal settlement who were identified by Dr Arun Mohan (Rotary Club of Tangasseri) at the Achankovil tribal settlement Medical Camp conducted by the Indian Medical Association, Kollam branch.
Rotary Club of Tangasseri Inaugurates $42,000 Global Grant Project "Save Kidney Save Life" with Donation of Dialysis Machines to Matha Hospital
Rotary Club of Tangasseri when its Global Grant Project "Save Kidney Save Life" worth 42,000 U.S $ was inaugurated by the District Governor Rtn Dr G.Sumithran The club donated four dialysis machines and related equipment to the Matha hospital and in return the club receives five dialysis every day totally free of cost for the patients recommended by the club for a period of five years.
KIMS Health Limb Centre and Rotary Club of Tangasseri Host Artificial Limb and Fitment Camp in TKM College of Arts and Science
Join us at the Artificial Limb and Fitment Camp, organized by KIMS Health Limb Centre in collaboration with the Rotary Club of Tangasseri. Transforming lives with custom prosthetics and expert care, this initiative brings hope and mobility to those in need.

Rotary Club Tangasserie Book Donation Campaign: Empowering Communities through Education
The Rotary Club Tangasserie is organizing a book donation campaign to provide educational resources to underserved communities. By donating gently used or new books, you can help enrich the lives of children and adults alike, fostering learning and growth for a brighter future.
